A SYMPHONY OF STYLE AND TRADITION

Nestled near the shoreline, half a kilometer from Mayajaal, Ritam is a sprawling destination venue spread across over 40 grounds, blending contemporary sophistication with timeless tradition.
Be it a lavish wedding or an intimate gathering, every facet of Ritam is curated to provide the perfect canvas for crafting unforgettable memories.

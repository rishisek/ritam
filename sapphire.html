The Sapphire

  1. Home
  2. Spaces
  3. The Sapphire

The Sapphire is a sophisticated, versatile event space designed to comfortably accommodate up to 100 guests for intimate events. Its fully air-conditioned interior boasts an elegant, adaptable palette designed to seamlessly transform to your theme and style.

The Sapphire includes a charming terrace framed by a revered Nagalinga tree and serves as an idyllic outdoor backdrop for smaller, culturally rich events such as Mehendi and Haldi ceremonies. The terrace opens to a mesmerizing view on the east, where the sapphire sea meets the beauty of the seamless sky. To the west, it overlooks a stunning poolside creating a sparkling sapphire glow.

  • Capacity 100 pax.
  • Area (Hall) 1000 sq. ft.
  • Area (Terrace) 1000 sq. ft.

The Bungalow

  1. Home
  2. Spaces
  3. The Bungalow

A stunning 6000 sqft ode to the vibrant artistry and heritage of India, The Bungalow is a beautifully designed space that celebrates India's diverse cultural tapestry.

The exquisite Chettinad foyer showcases the intricate craftsmanship of South India. Adjacent, the living and dining rooms echo the colors and motifs of renowned Indian artists like Raja Ravi Varma and Sadananadan, their masterpieces adorning the walls.

Each bedroom is a harmonious blend of the East and West coasts, where décor and accents display the rich diversity of India's coastal regions. The walls are a canvas to a curated collection of Indian art forms that include Madhubani, Patachitra, Saura and Pichwai.

The bridal suite, complete with an adjacent terrace offers a breathtaking 360-degree view of the venue and tantalizing glimpse of the ocean. The regal poster bed and the beautiful décor from across India sets the stage for unforgettable moments.

The patio, enclosed by gallery windows, is a green niche for intimate rituals like Vratham and Oonjal. Outside, a sprawling verandah, wrapped around the house, invites relaxation with traditional Kerala backrests, offering a tranquil space to unwind.

The Bungalow is a culturally rich space designed as a tribute to India's artistic heritage, offering a journey of discovery and delight at every turn.
